About Us

SRI SUGAM HOSPITAL

Sri Sugam Hospital is a trusted healthcare establishment located in the heart of Sholinganallur, Chennai, serving patients with dedicated medical attention 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Situated conveniently on IT Highway, our facility is equipped to handle a wide range of medical needs — from routine consultations to emergency care — ensuring that quality healthcare is always within reach for our community.

At Sri Sugam Hospital, we believe that every patient deserves to be treated with dignity, respect, and genuine compassion. Our team of medical professionals and support staff are committed to providing a safe and caring environment for every individual who walks through our doors. We continuously strive to improve our services and uphold the highest standards of patient care for the families of Chennai.
